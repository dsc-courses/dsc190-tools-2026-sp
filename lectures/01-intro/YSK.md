# The `ls` command

- What does the `-a` flag do in the `ls` command?
    - **Answer**: shows all files, including hidden files (starting with a dot)

- What does the `-l` flag do in the `ls` command?
    - **Answer**: shows files in long format (permissions, owner, size, date, name)

- What does the `-r` flag do in the `rm` command?
    - **Answer**: removes directories and their contents recursively
